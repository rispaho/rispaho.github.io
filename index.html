Nairobi is the capital and the largest city of Kenya. The name comes from the Maasai phrase Enkare Nyrobi, which translates to "cool water", a reference to the Nairobi River which flows through the city. It is commonly referred to as The Green City in the Sun.

Nairobi is proud to be the only city in the world with a national park. One of the town's top attractions is the Nairobi National Park, which is located 7 kilometers south of the city.
